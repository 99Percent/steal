These pages document the behavior of the steal.js module loader. steal.js packages and adds to two other projects:

  • ES6ModuleLoader - Provides the [Loader] and [System] Polyfill.
  • SystemJS - Provides most System extensions like System.paths, System.map and the AMD and CommonJS syntaxes.

steal.js improves workflows by adding conventional behavior, CSS and LESS plugins, and the steal syntax.
